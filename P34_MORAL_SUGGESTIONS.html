MORAL_SUGGESTIONS
Forgiveness is a deliberate attempt made by an information processing agent to cancel any retaliatory response towards some perceived source of malicious behavior (especially intentionally malicious behavior) in order to minimize further net harm (and not to reinforce the malicious behavior's future recurrence). For further elaboration on the topic of morality, visit the QUALITY_OF_LIFE_METRICS and TURING_COMPLETE_PHILOSOPHY pages of this website.
